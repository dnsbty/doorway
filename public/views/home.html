How It Works

1. Set up your bank account

We collect your bank details so payments can be automatically sent to your account.

2. Add your properties

Input addresses, rent amounts, and other information about your properties into the system.

3. Invite your tenants

Add your tenants to a property, and they will receive an email invitation. They will store their bank account information and receive a notification when it's time to pay each month.

4. Sit Back and Relax

After that, the system runs by itself. Rent payments will arrive directly to your bank account each month.
